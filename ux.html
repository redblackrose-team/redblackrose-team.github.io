Introduction to UX Design

What is UX Design?

User experience (UX) design is the process of making products or services useful, easy to use, and delightful to interact with. It’s about enhancing the experience that people have while interacting with your product and making sure they find value in what you’re providing.

Who is this for?

This roadmap is for anyone who wants to learn how to create products that are easy and enjoyable to use, from aspiring designers to product managers and developers.

Project Ideas

Beginner

  • Redesign the user flow of a popular app.
  • Create a wireframe and prototype for a simple mobile app.

Intermediate

  • Conduct user research and design a new feature for an existing product.
  • Design a website for a local business.

Advanced

  • Design a complex application from scratch, including user research, wireframing, and prototyping.
  • Conduct a usability study on an existing website and provide recommendations for improvement.
